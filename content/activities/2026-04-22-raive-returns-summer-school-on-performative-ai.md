The Algorithmic Gaze and CREATIE, two Antwerp-based research groups, join forces once again to organise RAIVE, a summer school focused on performative AI and interdisciplinary creation. Following two successful editions, the programme returns from 31 August to 6 September at the Royal Conservatory of Antwerp.

RAIVE brings together a small group of emerging artists to explore the intersections of performance, technology, and artistic practice. While the open call has now closed, more information about the programme can be found at: <https://raive.school/>
