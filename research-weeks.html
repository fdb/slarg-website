Research weeks

The SLARG research weeks present a view on the plurality of the research projects, processes, methods, experiments and propositions through workshops, presentations, seminars, performances, guided tours, and much more.

To discover what is happening at SLARG on an ongoing basis, we invite you to visit our research weeks, where you will encounter research and creation as entangled processes. Because research in the arts can be challenging to grasp at first sight, we organize guided tours and events led by the researchers themselves, in which you are welcome to participate.
