description: >-
  Multi Scripts – Blended Type Family Stories is a collective
  research-in-progress about multi-script typography. Partial and situated
  rather than universalist, it aims to be reflective, critical, speculative, and
  rich in proposals. It uses decolonial and feminist tools, it centres on
  diasporic experiences, pays attention to personal, professional, and family
  stories and anecdotes, to bring a contemporary reflection on multi-script
  typographic creation today
