Initiated and curated by Sint Lucas Antwerp and [Stadscanvas](https://www.fameus.be/stadscanvas)

Project week at [Rataplan](https://rataplanvzw.be/), guided by [Joana Rossi](https://www.instagram.com/atelierrojo/)

Textiles as a vehicle and a medium in an intercultural world. 
Participatory approaches where sociology and ecology converge. 

We weave together narrative threads and reflect on social fabrics. How can we interact with one another—and with textiles—in a sustainable way?

With students Sint Lucas Antwerp  participants & [Stadscanvas](https://www.fameus.be/stadscanvas)
