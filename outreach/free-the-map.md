Sint Lucas Antwerp, [Stadscanvas,](https://www.fameus.be/stadscanvas) [De Vormforesen](https://devormforensen.nl/en/)

A map is a visual story of the world. It feeds our imagination and shapes our view of the world. A standard atlas, however, predominantly tells only one story, that of the nation-state. Free the Map goes beyond the narrow, state-centric cartography and discusses several alternative cartographic representations of borders and migration.

Workshop by [Annelys de Vet](https://freethemap.org/) at Sint Lucas Antwerp.

With students Sint Lucas Antwerp participants & Stadscanvas.

Supported by [Ruth Razan Loos](https://slarg.be/people/ruth-razan-loos/) and Stadscanvas
