GROUNDS

Grounds is the renewed artistic research journal of Sint Lucas Antwerp. As the follow-up to TYPP, Grounds introduces a new editorial approach: editors become coaches, supporting researchers to experiment with publishing and open their artistic research to wider publics beyond academic communities.
