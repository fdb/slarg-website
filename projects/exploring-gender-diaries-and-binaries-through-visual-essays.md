In 2019, I began a series of mini sketchbooks as a private space to draw around fertility, trans pregnancy, chestfeeding, medical transition, and parenthood. In this project, I explore how to translate this archive into a collection of short visual essays. I work with the spontaneity and sequential structure of the sketches, experimenting with narrative styles to make the themes accessible. Conversations with other trans and queer authors help broaden the perspective.
